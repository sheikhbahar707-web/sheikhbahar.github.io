About Me

THE MIND BEHIND THE WORK

I'm Sheikh Bahar, a multidisciplinary creative specializing in Graphic Design, Motion Design, and Video Editing, with a BA (Honours) in Graphic Design & Multimedia from Shanto-Mariam University of Creative Technology.

I combine creative thinking, visual storytelling, and technical expertise to create work that is both aesthetically refined and strategically effective. Whether developing brand visuals, motion graphics, or video content, my focus is always on crafting experiences that capture attention, communicate purpose, and drive impact.

For me, great design isn't just about how it looks — it's about how it connects, communicates, and performs.
